Preparing for Your Service Appointment

To ensure a smooth and efficient service experience, here are some helpful tips before your scheduled appointment:

  • Clear access to your garden or outdoor area to allow our team to work safely.
  • Identify specific plants or areas you want to prioritize or discuss during service.
  • Inform us about any areas requiring special care or challenges, such as pest problems or irrigation issues.
  • Ensure pets and children are safely away from work areas during service.
  • Be available during or shortly after the appointment to answer any questions or provide feedback.
