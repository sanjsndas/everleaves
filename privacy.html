Privacy Policy

At Everleaves Garden Care, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our customers. The types of information we collect and hold include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, postal address, phone number, and other details you provide when you contact us or request services.
  • Technical Data: Information automatically collected when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and browsing behavior data.
  • Cookies and Tracking Data: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your user experience and gather information about website usage.
  • Payment Information: When making payments, your financial and billing details are securely collected by our payment processors; we do not store your card details on our servers.
  • Communications: Records of your communications with our customer service teams, including emails and calls.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in the following ways:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services effectively.
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our website and services.
  • To understand and analyze how you use our website and services.
  • To communicate with you, including responding to inquiries, sending service updates, and promotional offers.
  • To process payments and manage transactions securely.
  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues or fraud.
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners under strict confidentiality agreements for the purpose of providing services, including:

  • Service Providers: Such as payment processors, hosting services, and IT providers who assist with operating our website and service delivery.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to comply with a legal obligation, to protect our rights, or for safety reasons.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, sale, or acquisition of our business, your information may be transferred as part of the transaction under confidentiality clauses.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to distinguish you from other users. Cookies are small data files stored on your device to improve site functionality and user experience. We also use third-party analytics services to collect and analyze usage data.

You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings; however, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain website features.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information. These include encrypted transmissions (SSL/TLS), secure servers, access controls, and regular monitoring. Despite these efforts, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal data under applicable data protection laws, including:

  • Access: You may request information about the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Objection: You may object to the processing of your personal data for marketing or profiling.
  • Data Portability: You may request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond in accordance with applicable law.

7. Third-Party Websites and Services

Our website may contain links to external sites or services that are not operated by us. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of every site you visit.

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently obtained such data, please contact us immediately so we can delete it. Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children’s online activities and usage of websites and services.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Where feasible, we anonymize data to maintain confidentiality.

12. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection regulations and use appropriate safeguards for the protection of your personal information.

13. Consent

By using our website or services, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described within this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our site or provide your personal information.

14. Your Privacy Responsibilities

You are responsible for safeguarding any personal information or passwords that you use to access our site or services. Please ensure you provide accurate data, and notify us promptly of any changes.

15. Legal Bases for Processing Personal Data

Where applicable (such as under the GDPR), our legal bases for processing your personal data include:

  • Performance of a contract (e.g., to provide the services you request)
  • Compliance with a legal obligation
  • Your consent (which you may withdraw at any time)
  • Our legitimate interests in operating and improving our business.

16. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

We do not use automated decision-making processes or profiling that produce legal or similarly significant effects on you.

17. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). These include the right to request information about data collection and sharing practices, and to opt out of the sale of personal information, if applicable.

18. Additional Information

For more detailed information or specific inquiries about our Privacy Policy, data handling, or security practices, please contact our Privacy Officer at the above contact details.

Thank you for trusting Everleaves Garden Care with your personal information. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and maintaining your confidence.
