Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains how Everleaves Garden Care ("we", "us", and "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit or use our website. Your privacy and trust are very important to us. Please read carefully to understand what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide functionality, and improve user experience. Cookies can also help website owners analyze how visitors interact with their site.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use several different types of cookies on our website, including:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off. They enable basic functions such as page navigation and access to secure areas.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often. We use this data to improve the website's performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enable the website to remember choices you make, such as language preferences or login information, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant ads to you and limit the number of times you see an ad. They also help measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

3. How We Use Cookies

By using our website, cookies help us:

  • Recognize you as a returning visitor and provide personalized content.
  • Remember your preferences and user settings for a seamless experience.
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns to improve performance.
  • Ensure security and detect fraudulent activity.
  • Provide targeted advertisements and marketing.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies used on our site are set by third-party services, such as Google Analytics, advertising partners, and social media platforms. These third parties may collect information about your online activities across different websites. We do not control these cookies and encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information.

5. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You can control or block cookies by adjusting your browser settings. You may:

  • Accept or reject cookies entirely.
  • Delete cookies stored on your device.
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is placed.

However, please note that disabling some cookies may affect the functionality and user experience of our website.

7. Cookies We Set

  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted to remember preferences across visits.
  • Analytics Cookies: Track website usage to help us understand visitor interactions.
